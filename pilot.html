Five Steps to Become a Pilot !

  1. Choose a Flying School

    2. Selecting a DGCA Approved Flying School/Club. CAE Oxford Academy in Gondia, Maharashtra is one of the best aviation institute in the world.

  2. Explore The courses

    3. Determine which programme best suits you, based on your desired training route and on the necessary eligibility requirements. If you want to get CPL or PPL or ATPL.

  3. Apply and register

    4. Submit your online application by following the details in the Apply section of your preferred programme. Make sure you meet the eligibility requirements before applying.

  4. Get a Class 1 Medical Certificate

    5. From a medical examiner approved by the relevant national aviation authority (in some cases this is secured at the time of assessment).

  5. Pass Assesment and Selection

    6. If you meet all the eligibility requirements of your preferred programme, you will be invited to begin the assessment and selection process, which typically consists of a computer-based aptitude test and an in-person interview.
